Step 2 of 6

Exercise Instructions

Read these carefully before proceeding.

Getting into position

  1. Kneel and place forearms on the floor, elbows shoulder-width apart, directly under shoulders.
  2. Extend your legs behind you, toes on the floor, feet hip-width apart.
  3. Lift your hips so only forearms and toes support your body. Form a straight line head-to-heels.

During the hold

  • Tuck chin, relax shoulders and jaw.
  • Breathe steadily — do not hold your breath.
  • Keep hips level — no sagging or piking.

When to stop

Stop when you voluntarily quit OR when your form breaks (knees touch ground, or hips drop/rise and cannot be corrected within 2 seconds). Stop the timer immediately when the plank ends.

The music will start in the next step. There will be a short lead-in during which you will answer a few quick questions — then you will start the plank.
